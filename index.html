A Few Words About Us

The Algoma University Students’ Union Food Pantry is a collective-run student service dedicated to addressing food security within our AU community. We provide essential non-perishable and perishable food items to students through generous donations.

safety first

Learn all about the importance of hygiene and health safety. Discover practical tips for keeping your food clean and safe, ensuring the well-being of you and your loved ones.

Want to make a contribution to our community?

An in-kind donation can be dropped off from 9 AM to 6 PM from Monday to Friday by contacting ausufood.pantry@ausu.algomau.ca

Volunteering

Come and join our food pantry team as volunteers by filling this FORM or visit the Food Pantry between Monday - Saturday between 10 am to 5 PM. ALL SUPPORT IS WELCOMED and GREATLY APPRECIATED!

Nutrition Education

Join us in exploring the essentials of healthy eating and nutrition. Learn how to make delicious, balanced meals and discover tips for a healthier, more vibrant lifestyle.

Certified and trained team

Our team is well-trained and certified, combining expertise in food safety and nutrition to offer our community excellent service with a friendly touch.

happy environment

Learn how to make eco-friendly choices and contribute to a healthier planet. Discover simple ways to reduce waste and conserve resources for a sustainable future.

What We Offer

Our two main goals are to provide culturally relevant food that is locally sourced and highly nutritious. We believe that food is a significant way to make our students feel comfortable and welcome in their environment. We strive to include nutrient-dense items such as:

- Fresh produce and herbs (from the People’s Garden and community partners)
- Frozen foods (vegetables)
- Perishables (milk, bread, yogurt, eggs)
- Non-perishables (beans, pasta, rice, tuna, flour)
- Spices
- Condiments
- Oil
- Hygiene products
